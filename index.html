Who Are We?

Welcome to SPOORTHI FOUNDATION! We are a non-profit organization established in 2023 with a simple yet powerful mission to provide education, health, food and sports to deprived children, Spoorthi is not just an organization, its mission is to creative and enhance education for children, skill for youth and women for their self reliant, nurture their creativity and instil confidence in them....Our aim is to help marginalized people who are not able to educate themselves. We are always ready to help those people who are facing real-life challenges, of their.We are more than an organization; we are a community of like-minded individuals united by a shared vision of positive change. Together, we can build a brighter future. Join us in our journey to create a world where everyone has the chance to thrive and where compassion knows no bounds.
